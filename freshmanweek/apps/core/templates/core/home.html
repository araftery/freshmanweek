About Freshman Week

Welcome to the official website of the Opening Days for the Class of 2018! Freshman Week, as it is commonly called, begins on move-in day and extends through the first week of classes. It is the official welcome and orientation to Harvard. The Crimson Key Society is responsible for planning and hosting all of the social events during Freshman Week. Many of these events, such as the showings of Love Story and the First Chance Dance, are long-standing traditions.

During Freshman Week, make sure to stop by the Information Tent in Harvard Yard! Footballs and Spikeball sets are available for rent from the tent, and Class of 2018 t-shirts and Love Story tickets are available for purchase with a credit card.
